Review

Grand Theft Auto IV revolutionized open-world storytelling with a gritty and realistic depiction of Liberty City. The game blends cinematic narrative with freedom of choice, creating one of Rockstar’s most immersive experiences. The driving and shooting mechanics remain engaging, while the city feels alive with random events and side activities.

The protagonist, Niko Bellic, is one of the most memorable characters in the series, and the moral dilemmas add weight to your decisions. Graphically, it may show its age today, but the atmosphere and soundtrack make up for it.

About the Game

Released in 2008, GTA IV was developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It’s available on PC, Xbox 360, and PlayStation 3. The game focuses on crime, loyalty, and survival in a sprawling open-world city.
